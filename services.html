No templates. No catalogue parts. No off-the-shelf props. Every Vitrine Taj display is custom designed and custom built — from the first sketch to the final screw. Here's what that looks like.

Service 01

Kinetic & Automated Displays

Motion is the most powerful tool in window display. A still image competes with every other static thing on the street. A moving display wins every time.

We engineer custom kinetic mechanisms — rotating elements, oscillating figures, rising and falling components, synchronized multi-part sequences — that bring your window to life 24 hours a day.

Every mechanism is designed, built, and tested in-house. We choose materials for beauty and durability, and size motors for quiet, reliable long-term operation.

Custom-engineered motion — no off-the-shelf kits
Silent, continuous operation — runs all day, every day
Single and multi-element choreography
Timer and sensor integration available

Service 02

3D Printed Sculpture & Props

3D printing lets us make things that simply can't be bought — sculptural pieces designed entirely around your brand, with forms, details, and textures that are impossible to source anywhere else.

We model everything from scratch and print in-house, which means we control every dimension, every curve, every surface finish. From delicate filigree jewellery display props to bold architectural statement pieces — we can build it.

Pieces can be painted, finished, gilded, or left raw depending on the aesthetic. Many designs incorporate motion and work in combination with our kinetic systems.

Fully original designs — modelled from scratch for you
In-house printing — full quality control
Multiple materials and finishes
Integrates with kinetic systems

Painted Art & Illustration

A hand-painted backdrop does something no print or screen can replicate — it signals craft, care, and originality in a way customers immediately feel, even if they can't say why.

Our original painted and illustrated work creates the atmosphere and depth that elevates a collection of elements into a unified display. Whether it's a full backdrop scene, painted glass panels, illustrated figures, or decorative details — every brushstroke is in service of the story.

Service 04

Seasonal & Campaign Displays

Your window should change. A display that looks the same in February and October is a missed opportunity — and a signal to regular passersby that nothing is happening inside.

We design and build seasonal refreshes and campaign-specific displays that keep your storefront feeling current, surprising, and alive all year round. Holiday windows, launch events, themed seasons — we plan it all with you.

Full seasonal planning — we map the year with you
Modular systems that swap efficiently
