Our Process

From first hello
to opening night.

We've designed our process so that you always know what's happening, nothing is a surprise, and the end result exceeds what you imagined. Here's exactly how it works.

Step 01

Discovery
Call

01

Every project starts with a conversation — no brief required, no forms to fill in. We just talk. We want to understand your shop, your customers, what makes you different, and what feeling you want your window to create.

We'll ask questions that might surprise you. Not "what colours do you like" but "what's the first thing you want someone to feel when they stop outside your shop at 8pm?"

What we cover
Your brand, your customers, the story you want to tell, the feeling you want to create, your window dimensions, and any constraints.
How long it takes
Usually 45–60 minutes over video call or in-person if you're local. Relaxed, no pressure.
What you'll leave with
A clear sense of whether we're the right fit, and a timeline for receiving your initial concept proposal.
Step 02

Concept &
Design

02

We go away and develop your concept from scratch — mood boards, colour palette, compositional sketches, material choices, and motion storyboards. Every element is considered in relation to every other element.

You receive a full concept presentation that shows you exactly what we plan to build, how it will move, and how it will feel. Nothing is built until you approve it. Revisions are part of the process — we get it right before we build.

What you receive
Mood boards, material samples, motion descriptions, 3D visualisations, and a full written brief for the display.
Revisions
We include two rounds of revisions. Most projects are approved within the first or second round.
Timeline
Concept delivery within 1–2 weeks of discovery call, depending on project scope.
Step 03

Build &
Fabricate

03

Once the concept is approved, we build. Every element is designed, fabricated, printed, painted, and assembled by us — nothing is outsourced, nothing is bought off a shelf and repurposed.

We send you progress updates throughout — photos and short videos as things come together. You never have to wonder what's happening. We also do thorough motion testing and quality checks before anything leaves our studio.

What's built in-house
3D modelling, printing, painting, mechanism engineering, electronics, assembly, and finishing. Everything.
Progress updates
Regular photo and video updates so you can see your display coming to life before delivery.
Timeline
Build time varies by project complexity — typically 3–8 weeks from concept approval.
Step 04

Install &
Launch

04

We come to you. We handle the full installation — everything secured, positioned, motion tested, and running before we leave. We'll walk you through how to operate and maintain your display, and leave you with written care instructions.

This is the moment the window comes alive in your shop — and we love being there for it. Most installations take half a day to a full day depending on complexity.

What's included
Full on-site installation, motion testing, positioning adjustments, operational handover, and written care guide.
After install
We're available for questions and support. Maintenance visits and seasonal swap-outs can be arranged as ongoing service.
The moment
We switch it on together. There's nothing better than seeing it running in your window for the first time.

FAQs

Questions we
hear a lot.

Do I need to know what I want before contacting you? +
Not at all. Most of our clients come to us with a feeling rather than a brief — "I want it to feel magical" or "I want people to stop and try to figure out how it works." That's more than enough to start. The concept development process is where the specifics emerge.
My shop window is small. Does that matter? +
Not at all. Some of the most striking displays we've conceived are for small windows — constraint often produces the most creative work. We design for the space you have.
How much does a display cost? +
We don't publish pricing because every project is different — a single sculptural piece differs significantly from a full kinetic installation with painted backdrops. We'll give you a clear quote after the discovery call, once we understand your vision and scope. We work with independent shops and price accordingly.
Do you travel for installations? +
Yes. We're available to travel for installation depending on location and project size. Get in touch and we'll figure it out together.
Can I update or refresh my display later? +
Absolutely — in fact, we'd love to plan this with you from the start. We design modular systems where possible so that seasonal refreshes are efficient and cost-effective. We offer ongoing maintenance and swap-out services for all our clients.
What if I want to change the concept after approval? +
Changes are easier to accommodate early. We include two revision rounds during concept stage specifically so we land on the right design before building. Major changes after build sign-off may affect timeline and cost — we'll always be transparent about this.
