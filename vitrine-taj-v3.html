Our Story

Built by two
engineers who
fell in love
with a window.

We're a couple of engineers and artists — one a painter, both tinkerers — who spent years designing, 3D printing, and building projects from the ground up just for the joy of it.

Travelling through Europe, something stopped us in our tracks: the shop windows. Paris, Florence, Amsterdam, Lisbon — everywhere we went, small boutiques had these incredible, living displays that made you feel something before you even stepped inside. Wonder. Curiosity. The irresistible pull to get closer.

We came home and couldn't stop thinking: why don't shops here feel like that? So we decided to do something about it. Vitrine Taj was born to bring that magic back — one window at a time.

How It Works

From first conversation
to opening night.

Step 01

Discovery Call

We learn about your shop, your customers, your brand, and the story you want your window to tell. No brief needed — just a conversation.

Step 02

Concept & Design

We develop mood boards, sketches, and 3D previews of your display. You see and approve everything before we build a single piece.

Step 03

Build & Fabricate

We fabricate all elements in our studio — printing, painting, engineering, and assembling every component by hand. Progress updates throughout.

Step 04

Install & Launch

We come to you, set up everything, test all motion and lighting, and hand over a window that's ready to run. Ongoing support always available.
