Display Ideas

Displays we're
dying to build.

We have notebooks full of concepts — displays we've sketched, mechanisms we've prototyped, ideas we've been waiting for the right client to bring to life. Here's a taste of what's possible.

🌸

The Slow Bloom

A kinetic display of 3D printed flowers that slowly open and close over the course of the day — perfect for a florist or botanical shop. Mechanical petals, no electronics required.

⌛

The Antique Orrery

A rotating solar system of spheres and rings — reimagined in a boutique's brand colours. Hypnotic, mechanical, endlessly watchable. Works for jewellery, gifts, or any shop with a sense of wonder.

📖

The Living Story

A painted scene where a single element — a bird, a leaf, a figure — moves through the composition on a loop. Part painting, part theatre. Ideal for bookshops, gift stores, or independent galleries.

💎

The Faceted Light

Dozens of 3D printed geometric facets slowly rotating in a jewellery store window, catching and scattering light throughout the day. No two moments look the same.
