Our Story

Born from a trip.
Built from scratch.

We're a couple of engineers and artists — one a painter, both lifelong tinkerers — who have spent years designing, 3D printing, and building projects from the ground up simply because we love making things.

Between us we bring together mechanical engineering, fine art painting, software, AI, and client-facing consulting. We know how to design something beautiful. We know how to build it so it actually works. And we know how to listen to a client and translate what they want into something real.

Vitrine Taj is the place where all of that comes together — the intersection of engineering and art, applied to the most overlooked real estate in retail: the shop window.

Travelling through
Europe, something
kept stopping us.
The windows.

From Paris to Florence, Amsterdam to Lisbon — everywhere we went, small independent boutiques had these incredible, living window displays that made you feel something before you even stepped inside.

Wonder. Curiosity. That irresistible pull to get closer, to stop, to look. Sometimes you'd stand there for minutes just to figure out how it worked or what it was trying to say.

We came home and couldn't stop thinking: why don't shops here feel like that? The craft, the storytelling, the theatre of a beautiful window — it seemed like a lost art.

So we decided to revive it. Vitrine Taj exists to bring that magic back — one window, one shop, one story at a time.

What We Believe

A few things we
refuse to compromise on.

01
Everything custom. Always.
We will never sell you a template or a pre-made prop. Every single display is designed and built from scratch for your shop, your brand, and your story. That's not a selling point — it's the entire point.
02
The window is a stage.
We treat every window like a theatre set — every element serves the story. Colour, light, motion, texture, scale: nothing is accidental. Every decision earns its place in the composition.
03
Small shops deserve great design.
The best independent boutiques in Paris and Florence don't have million-dollar budgets — they have passion, craft, and a commitment to the customer experience. We exist to serve that kind of shop.
04
Curiosity is the point.
The best displays don't just look nice — they make people stop, tilt their head, and wonder. We design for that moment of pause. That's the magic we're chasing with every project.
